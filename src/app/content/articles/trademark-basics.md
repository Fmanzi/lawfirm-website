Trademarks are essential to brand identity. This article explains clearance searches, registration, enforcement, and maintaining rights over time.

## Registration Process

- Conduct clearance searches
- File application in chosen jurisdictions
- Monitor and enforce against infringers

Proactive brand protection reduces the risk of costly disputes later.
