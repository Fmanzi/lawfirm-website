Employers expanding internationally face compliance challenges including payroll, benefits, tax withholding, and immigration status. This article highlights core risks and mitigation strategies.

## Practical Steps

- Map applicable local laws
- Use local entities or EORs
- Document working arrangements and tax responsibilities

Careful planning avoids costly misclassification and compliance problems.
