Fintech companies operate at the intersection of finance and technology, facing rules on payments, data, licensing, and anti-money laundering. This roadmap outlines where to begin and common pitfalls.

## Core Areas

- Licensing and sandbox regimes
- AML/KYC obligations
- Consumer protection and disclosures

Early regulatory engagement reduces friction and supports sustainable growth.
