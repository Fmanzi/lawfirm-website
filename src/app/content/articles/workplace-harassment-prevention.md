Employers must create clear policies, provide training, and establish reporting mechanisms to prevent workplace harassment. This piece outlines actionable steps and best practices.

## Key Actions

- Draft clear anti-harassment policies
- Conduct regular training
- Maintain confidential reporting channels

Early intervention and strong culture reduce legal risk and improve workplace well-being.
