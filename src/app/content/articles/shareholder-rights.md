Shareholders are fundamental stakeholders in any company. This article outlines typical shareholder rights, mechanisms for enforcement, and best practices for governance that protect minority investors.

## Core Rights

- Voting rights
- Access to information
- Dividend rights

Companies should adopt transparent policies to ensure shareholders can exercise their rights effectively.
